Biography

I am a recent graduate in BTech (equivalent to BS in USA) Computer Science and Engineering from Swami Vivekananda Institute of Science and Technology college affiliated to the West Bengal University of Technology with an average GPA of 8.28 over all the eight semesters.

My research interest lies in the area of Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, Human-Computer Interaction, Information Visualization, and Databases. I am always interested in knowing how a computer does what it does. More recently, I started exploring different hybrid machine learning models to analyze their performance with solar power forecasting, and also, analyzing the impact on various aspects of our daily lives due to the pandemic situation by means of statistical modeling techniques. As a core member of the IEEE and Computer Society of India organizations, I organized and took part in several technical events (webinars, online learning etc).
